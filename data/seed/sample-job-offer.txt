Embedded Software Engineer — Connected Devices
Acme Robotics · Lausanne, Switzerland · Full-time

We are looking for an embedded software engineer to design and ship firmware
for our next generation of industrial IoT sensors. You will work close to the
hardware, debug real boards, and own features from schematic to shipped product.

Required skills
- 2+ years writing firmware in C or C++ on ARM Cortex-M (STM32 or Nordic nRF)
- Familiarity with BLE, SPI, I2C, CAN, RS232
- Comfortable with electronics debugging: oscilloscope, logic analyzer, JTAG/SWD
- Strong understanding of RTOS concepts (FreeRTOS preferred)
- Git, code review, basic CI

Nice to have
- Experience with Docker and Linux servers
- Cloud/IoT data pipelines (InfluxDB, Grafana)
- Industrial protocols / PLC integration
- A second language (Python, Go, TypeScript) for tooling
- Robotics / mechatronics background

You will
- Bring up new boards and write production firmware
- Build calibration and test tooling
- Integrate devices with our cloud stack
- Collaborate with hardware, mechanical, and product teams

Bonus points if you have led a small project end to end, contributed to
open-source, or built something cool just for fun.
